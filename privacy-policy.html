Privacy Poliy For Bitteller

At Bitteller, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect data when you use our global payment solutions platform and related services. By accessing or using our Platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide, improve, and secure our services. This includes:

* Personal Information:DOB, BVN (We don't store these)
* Transactional Data:Details of payments, and recipient information.
* Technical Data:IP address, device information for Push notifications
* User Credentials:Username, password, and authentication details for Platform access.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

* Process and facilitate inter platform and intra platform payments.
* Verify user identity and comply with legal/regulatory obligations (e.g., anti-money laundering checks).
* Improve Platform functionality, security, and user experience.
* Communicate with you about transactions, updates, and support inquiries.
* Analyze trends to optimize payment solutions and business operations.
* Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, or misuse of the Platform.

Sharing Information

We use your data to:

1. Service Providers: Trusted third parties (e.g., banks, payment processors, auditors) who assist in delivering our services.
2. Legal Compliance:To comply with laws, regulations, or legal requests (e.g., tax authorities, law enforcement).
3.With Your Consent:For purposes explicitly authorized by you.
4.Business Transfers:In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
5.With Your Consent:For purposes explicitly authorized by you
We do not sell your personal or business data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

* Encryption:Data transmitted via the Platform is secured using SSL/TLS encryption.
* Access Controls:Restricted access to sensitive data based on roles and responsibilities.
* Regular Audits:Security assessments to identify and address vulnerabilities.

International Data Transfer

As a nation wide payment solutions provider, your data may be transferred to and processed in systems outside our platform. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

Your Rights

Depending on your preferences, you may have the right to:

* Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
* Object to or restrict processing of your data.
* Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.Withdraw consent (where applicable).
* Withdraw consent (where applicable).To exercise these rights, contact us at info@bitteller.com.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tools to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

Retention of Data

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on our Platform with an updated “Last Revised” date. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
