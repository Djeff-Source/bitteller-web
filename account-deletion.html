How to Submit a Deletion Request

1. Through Your Account (Recommended):

* Log in to your PerryLeo account.
* Navigate to Settings > Privacy & Security > Delete Account.
* Follow the prompts to confirm permanent deletion.

2. Via email:

* Send a deletion request to privacy@perryleo.com from your registered email address.
* Include the subject line: “Request for Account Deletion”.
* Provide your full name, business name (if applicable), and account ID (if available).

3. By Contacting Support:

* Reach out to our support team at support@bitteller.com or +234 *** *** ***
* Verify your identity to process the request.
* Provide your full name, business name (if applicable), and account ID (if available).

Important Notes

Irreversible Action: Deletion cannot be undone. Ensure you download or back up critical data before proceeding.
